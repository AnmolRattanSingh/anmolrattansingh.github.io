Research Interests

Progress in Machine Learning

This research can help aid researchers in understanding trends and developing more efficient and scalable machine learning models.

  • @ MIT FutureTech Lab, I am collaborating with cross-functional teams on literature reviews and surveys to research various algorithms and establish metrics to classify and quantify progress in machine learning. This is for the Algorithm Wiki project.

Decentralized Digital Identity

This research is paving the way for a globally interconnected digital identity ecosystem and addresses critical challenges such as cross-border compatibility, privacy preservation, and user-centric control over personal data.

  • @ MIT Connection Science, I helped create a framework for the interoperability of verifiable credentials and personal data stores. Helped improve the practicality of verifiable credentials.
  • @ MOSIP, I upgraded the bluetooth credential exchange module of their decentralized mobile wallet. Got hands on experience with implementing decentralized technologies.
